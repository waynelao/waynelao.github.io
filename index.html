About me

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor. My research focuses on designing control system for Ocean Wave Energy Coverters (OWECs).

I received the B.S. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Tongji University at 2015. I reveived M.S. in Civi Engineering at 2017 and M.S. in Electrical Engineering at 2020, both from the Univeristy of Michigan at Ann Arbor.
